Privacy Policy for Yest Ride-Hailing Platform

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Yest ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Yest ride-hailing platform through two mobile applications:

  • YEST Driver: For drivers providing transportation services
  • YesTaxi: For riders requesting transportation services

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use either of our applications for the Yest ride-hailing platform in Central Asia.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

For YEST Driver App:

  • Account Information: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Driver Documents: Driver's license, vehicle registration, profile photo
  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, color, plate number
  • Financial Information: Wallet balance, transaction history
  • Identification: Gender (for optional gender-based ride matching)

For YesTaxi Rider App:

  • Account Information: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, saved addresses (home, work)
  • Payment Information: Trip payment records and referral bonus balance
  • Preferences: Gender preference for driver matching (optional)
  • Trip History: Past rides, favorite drivers, saved places

2.2 Location Data

For YEST Driver App:

  • Real-time Location: Continuously collected while you are online to match you with nearby riders and navigate to destinations
  • Background Location: Collected during active trips even when the app is in the background to track trip progress
  • Trip History: Pickup and drop-off locations for completed trips

For YesTaxi Rider App:

  • Current Location: Used to show your pickup location and find nearby drivers
  • Destination: The address you want to travel to
  • Trip History: Pickup and drop-off locations for your past rides
  • Foreground Only: Location is only collected when the app is actively in use

2.3 Usage Data

  • Trip statistics (total rides, acceptance rate, cancellation rate)
  • Ratings and reviews
  • App usage patterns and interactions
  • Device information and crash reports
  • Referral code usage and bonus tracking

4. How We Use Your Information

Common to Both Apps:

  • Service Delivery: To match riders with drivers, facilitate trips, and process payments
  • Safety & Security: To verify identities, ensure platform safety, and investigate incidents
  • Account Management: To maintain user accounts, transaction history, and preferences
  • Communication: To send ride notifications, updates, and important announcements
  • Analytics: To improve our service, optimize pricing, and enhance user experience
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

YEST Driver Specific:

  • Document Verification: To verify driver licenses, vehicle registration, and identity
  • Earnings Management: To calculate fares, manage wallet balance, and process payouts
  • Performance Tracking: To monitor acceptance rates, completion rates, and driver ratings

YesTaxi Rider Specific:

  • Ride Matching: To find and connect you with the nearest available driver
  • Referral Program: To track referral codes, calculate bonuses, and reward both parties
  • Saved Preferences: To remember your saved addresses, favorite drivers, and preferences

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

Between Users:

  • Driver to Rider: When a driver accepts a ride, the rider sees the driver's name, photo, vehicle details, location, and rating
  • Rider to Driver: When a ride is requested, the driver sees the rider's name, photo (if provided), pickup location, and rating

With Service Providers:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication services
  • Google Maps: Navigation, geocoding, and mapping services
  • Firebase: Push notifications and crash analytics

With Administrators:

  • Support Team: Can access your information to provide assistance, resolve issues, and handle disputes
  • Admin Panel: Used to manage driver approvals, monitor trips, and ensure platform quality

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law enforcement or government authorities
  • To protect rights, safety, and property of users and the platform
  • To comply with court orders or legal processes

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage with Supabase
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

7. Data Retention

  • Active Drivers: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Inactive/Deleted Accounts: Data is retained for 3 years for legal and safety purposes, then deleted
  • Trip Data: Trip history is retained for 5 years for accounting and compliance

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out: Disable non-essential notifications in app settings
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

9. Children's Privacy

  • YEST Driver: Only available to individuals who are at least 18 years old and hold a valid driver's license
  • YesTaxi Rider: Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account

We do not knowingly collect information from minors under 18 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. International Users

Yest primarily operates in Central Asia (Uzbekistan and neighboring countries). Your data is stored on servers that may be located in different countries. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations.
